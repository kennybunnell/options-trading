================================================================================
STRATEGY GUIDELINES
================================================================================

LEAP Selection:
- DTE: 270-450 days (9-15 months)
- Delta: 0.70-0.90 (deep ITM)
- Open Interest: 50+ (liquidity)

Short Call Selection:
- DTE: 30-45 days (theta decay sweet spot)
- Delta: <0.30 (low assignment probability)
- Strike: ABOVE LEAP strike (safety)
- Premium: $50+ per contract (worth the risk)

ROI Targets:
- Conservative: 50% (recover half of LEAP cost)
- Aggressive: 100% (recover full LEAP cost)
- Typical: 3-6 short calls to reach 50%

Roll Triggers:
- CRITICAL: ITM with ‚â§7 DTE ‚Üí Roll immediately
- HIGH: ITM with >7 DTE ‚Üí Monitor and prepare to roll
- MODERATE: Within 5% of strike ‚Üí Watch closely
- LOW: Safely OTM ‚Üí Let it ride
